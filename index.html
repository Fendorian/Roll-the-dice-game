Extermination count

There are 7.53 billion people on the Earth. Since there are that much people it is too hard to make life possible for everybody. So we came up with an idea. Idea is pretty simple, we believe that 70% people are supposed to be gone in order for the rest of 30% to life in normal conditions for future. In order to see who will be the one who will end up dead. We made a pretty simple simulation to choose who will move on. You simply click on the Roll button and you will face yourself with a destiny. If you roll from 1 to 4, you will be exterminated. But if you roll 5 or 6. You will move on your life normally. Good luck!
